Team Callisto's
Design Process

1Identify the Need

Researching the Needs

Don't just jump in blind, find ideas from multiple sources

  • About Face: Understand the Problem, Design Research
  • Research of Needs
    • User Interviews & Communication
    • Analytics Software
    • Self-Aware Need Identification (bursts of creativity)

Discovery of the Needs

Keep it Simple

  • Break up multiple features into multiple needs and processes
  • Each need should have a Goal, (About Face: Goal-Directed Design)

2Brainstorm Solutions Collectively

Understand the Project

You can't develop a product you don't understand

  • When we began this first project, we were all pretty much in the dark
    • Take time to understand the project
  • We want to make sure we understand the problem we are trying to solve

Research where the need may have been solved before

Don't Make Me Think: Don't Reinvent the Wheel

  • Research can be done by each team member individually before brainstorm collaboration

Collaborate on brainstorming ideas to meet the Goal

  • Pen and paper 'no-holds-barred freestyle' style
    • No ideas are bad ideas :^)

Refine the ideas

  • Prioritize which solutions come before others
  • Ask questions, further the discussion
    • Good questions lead to good ideas
  • Determine which solutions would be the most effective and go from there

3Draft Multiple Solutions for Need

Draft Multiple Solutions for Need

  • Use Ideas from Collective Brainstorm to create handwritten/quick drafts of solutions
    • This includes wireframes and sketches for interaction
  • Draw solutions together in person and on paper

Draft Multiple Solutions for Need

Draft Multiple Solutions for Need

  • Gather User Information on which drafts are the most effective at meeting the Goal of the Need
    • In person, so that they can see the sketches
  • Decide based on User Information which draft to proceed to Design with.

4Begin Designing from the Atomic Level

Designing from Atomic Level

Create realistic iterations of draft

Use Sketch/Figma/Illustrator/PhotoShop to do this

  • Work from small to large
    • Build work in small pieces and work up from there
  • Incorporate Atomic Design

Designing from Atomic Level

Use mockup prototyping software to incorporate interaction & motion

  • i.e. InvisionApp, Marvel, Principle, etc.
  • What it interaction?
    • Transitions from different design states that encourages the user to complete their task/goal
  • What is motion?
    • Adding motion shows how an app is organized and what it can do, as well as providies guided focus between views for the user
    • Google Material Motion

User Test Design Prototype

  • If: Prototype seems to be the most effective at solving the need, then proceed to development
  • Else: Repeat this step to redesign a different solution idea from brainstorm (or even go back to step 2 or 3)

5Begin Developing Incrementally

Begin development with Design & User Feedback in mind.

  • Test elements at every stage.
    • "If all the pistons are good, the engine will run well"
  • Division and collaboration based on project.

Comprehensive User Testing
of entire project

  • Return to any previous steps if reevaluation is necessary
  • If not...

6Submit. Celebrate! Then repeat for future needs
